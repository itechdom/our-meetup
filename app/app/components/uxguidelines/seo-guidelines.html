SEO Guidelines

Basic SEO Guidelines

  • As much as possible, leave written content as selectable text (do not flatten into an image)
  • Always add descriptive meta-tags to image assets.
  • Consistently use descriptive page titles, headers, and subtitles across your site.
  • Structure your URLs with navigational hierarchy and descriptive text in mind
  • Use concise and direct copy with a mind toward keywords for all written content
  • Actively promote your content with links to external outlets, such as social networks, blogs, and external press release and news outlets.
