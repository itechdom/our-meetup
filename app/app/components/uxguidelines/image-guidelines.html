Image Selection & Usage Guidelines

For image curation, select images that:

  • Are at least 1200px wide and 500px tall at 72ppi to support application in any area of the site on all devices.
  • Composed heavily of brand colors if for use in full color applications.
  • Contain primary subject matter in brand colors if for use in select color applications.
  • Contain a great amount of tonal variation, texture and contrast if for use in black and white applications.

For development consideration:

  • All images should be able to be placed as left justified, centered or right justified.
  • Resized fluidly across breakpoints.
