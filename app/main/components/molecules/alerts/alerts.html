Alerts & Notifications

Full Screen

A full screen alert will take over the whole page, forcing the user to acknowledge the alert before continuing on. The background is slightly opaque to view contents below the alert.

This design is the same across all breakpoints.

Tooltip

The tooltip alert has the same content styling as the Full Screen version but used without blocking the entire page. The arrow tip indicates the source of the tip.

On Mobile devices the tooltip will convert to a Full Screen alert because of the sizing.
